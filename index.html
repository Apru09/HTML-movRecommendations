Top 3 recommendations

By Prudhvishankar

  1. Demon Slayer aka Kimetsu no yaiba

    This is the best anime I've known ever. You get every emotion out of it and also the storyline of the anime is extremely exciting.

  2. Naruto OG

    I can't compare this to my first recommendation however, I can say that even this anime covers all the emotions, has amazing fights, and it gives you a ninja like feel.

  3. Cute Programmer

    This is my all time favourite and my first ever dramas. This is the story of a young girl who falls in love with a gaming company's owner. Things get interesting when both of them start liking each other and don't let their feelings out.
